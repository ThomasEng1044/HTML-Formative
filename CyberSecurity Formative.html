Cyber Security



What is Cyber Security

Cyber security is the protection of computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems and networks from cyber attacks which result in theft, damage or disruption to the services they provide.

Why is Cyber Security Important

Cyber security is important because as technology advances and we become more reliant on technology, cyber-attacks have become more frequent and sophisticated. Cyber-attacks can be very damaging to companies as they can result in financial loss, loss of data and loss of reputation. Individuals and small businesses are most in danger of cyber-attacks because they often do not have strong cyber security measures since they often do not have sufficient Cyber Security measures in place.
